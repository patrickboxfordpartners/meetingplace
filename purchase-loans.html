Common Questions

Purchase Loan FAQ

How much do I need for a down payment?

Down payment requirements vary by loan type. Conventional loans can require as little as 3%, FHA loans 3.5%, VA and USDA loans 0%, and jumbo loans typically 10-20%. Your specific situation and loan type will determine your requirement.

What credit score do I need to buy a home?

Minimum credit scores vary: Conventional loans typically require 620+, FHA loans 580+, VA loans around 620+, and jumbo loans 700+. However, higher scores often qualify for better interest rates.

How long does it take to close on a home?

On average, it takes 30-45 days to close on a home purchase. With all documents ready and a streamlined process, we can sometimes close in as little as 21-30 days.

What's the difference between pre-qualification and pre-approval?

Pre-qualification is an estimate based on self-reported information. Pre-approval is a more thorough process involving credit checks and document verification, giving you a stronger position when making an offer.

Can I use gift money for my down payment?

Yes! Most loan programs allow gift funds from family members for down payment and closing costs. You'll need a gift letter stating the funds are a gift and not a loan that needs to be repaid.
